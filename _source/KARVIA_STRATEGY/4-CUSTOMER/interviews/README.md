## Purpose

Store user research interview transcripts, synthesis documents, and research insights. This folder captures the deep qualitative understanding of customer needs, behaviors, and mental models.

## Owner

**Primary**: UX Research
**Secondary**: CPO, Product Designers

---

## Research Types

| Type | Purpose | Frequency |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Discovery | Understand problem space | Pre-feature |
| Usability | Test existing designs | Per release |
| Validation | Confirm solution fit | Post-release |
| Churn | Understand why they left | As needed |
