# Evidence: Product-Market Fit Evidence

<!-- @GENOME T3-NAV-005 | ACTIVE | 2026-04-01 | parent:T1-NAV-003 | auto:/strategy | linked:/audit -->

## Purpose

Collect and curate evidence of product-market fit, customer success stories, testimonials, and validation data. This is the "proof" folder for stakeholder communications and strategic decisions.

## Owner

**Primary**: CPO
**Secondary**: Marketing, Customer Success

---

## Evidence Types

| Type | Purpose | Used For |
|------|---------|----------|
| Testimonials | Customer quotes | Marketing, sales |
| Case studies | Success stories | Sales enablement |
| PMF signals | Quantitative validation | Investor updates |
| Competitive wins | Why they chose us | Positioning |
| Referrals | Organic growth proof | Growth metrics |

---

## Artifacts Stored Here

| Type | Naming | Example |
|------|--------|---------|
| Testimonial | `YYYY_MM_DD_TESTIMONIAL_[COMPANY].md` | `2026_04_01_TESTIMONIAL_ACME.md` |
| Case study | `YYYY_MM_DD_CASE_STUDY_[COMPANY].md` | `2026_04_01_CASE_STUDY_ACME.md` |
| PMF report | `YYYY_QN_PMF_EVIDENCE.md` | `2026_Q2_PMF_EVIDENCE.md` |

---

## PMF Signals Tracked

| Signal | Measurement | Target |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Organic referrals | % new customers from referral | >30% |
| Retention | 90-day retention rate | >85% |
| Expansion | % accounts upgrading | >20% |
| NPS | Net Promoter Score | >50 |
| "Must-have" | % saying "very disappointed" if gone | >40% |
