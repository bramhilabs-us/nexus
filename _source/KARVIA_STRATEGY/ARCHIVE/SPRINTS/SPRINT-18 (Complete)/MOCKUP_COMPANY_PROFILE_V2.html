Sprint 18 Design Changes Summary

Epic CP1: Maturity Visibility

  • • Maturity ring replaces simple % completion
  • • Stage progression dots (Discovery → Mastery)
  • • "What AI knows about you" summary panel
  • • Category progress cards (clickable navigation)

Epic CP2: Guided Completion

  • • Chip-based selections (less typing)
  • • "High AI Impact" badges on key fields
  • • Tooltips explaining "Why this matters"
  • • Smart suggestions based on industry

Epic CP3: Metrics Enhancement

  • • Quick input cards (inline editing)
  • • Visual metrics grid (not form fields)
  • • AI-driven insights/warnings
  • • Progressive disclosure for advanced metrics

Epic CP4: Vision Focus

  • • Priority #1 highlighted as MOST IMPORTANT
  • • Pre-built suggestions by industry
  • • Simple question flow (not forms)
  • • Direct connection to OKR generation
