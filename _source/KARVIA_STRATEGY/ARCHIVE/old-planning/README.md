## 💡 Planning Best Practices

**DO**:
- ✅ Update TODOs daily
- ✅ Mark completion dates
- ✅ Link to specific documents/issues
- ✅ Use clear, actionable language

**DON'T**:
- ❌ Leave stale items for weeks
- ❌ Use vague descriptions
- ❌ Duplicate tasks across files
- ❌ Forget to archive completed work
