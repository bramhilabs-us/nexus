**Who** struggle with alignment chaos and spreadsheet fatigue
**KARVIA Pro** is a lightweight OKR platform
**That** delivers enterprise-level structure with startup simplicity
**Unlike** enterprise tools (too complex, $300+/user) or spreadsheets (too brittle)
**KARVIA Pro** sets up in <7 days, costs 60% less, and offers optional AI superpowers via iBrain toggle

## 💡 Customer Value (Outcome Pillars)

### **1. Clarity**
**Customer Gets**: Know the next move, no guesswork

**Specific Value**:
- Top 3 company priorities visible on every dashboard
- Multi-level OKR cascade (Company → Department → Team → Individual)
- Clear "done" criteria (no ambiguity on success metrics)
- Progress indicators (on-track, at-risk, ahead, blocked)

**Quantified Impact**:
- Reduce "What should I prioritize?" questions by 80% (from 10x/week to 2x/week)
- <5 minute dashboard review vs 15+ hours in status meetings
- 95%+ team alignment score (vs 60% with spreadsheets)

### **2. Commitment**
**Customer Gets**: Stay the course, build follow-through

**Specific Value**:
- Weekly progress prompts (gentle nudges, not nagging)
- Milestone auto-detection (25%, 50%, 75%, 100% celebrations)
- Public recognition feed (peer accountability)
- Historical tracking (see trends, not just snapshots)

**Quantified Impact**:
- 70%+ OKR completion rate (vs 30% industry average)
- 2+ recognitions per user per month (vs 0.3 with ad-hoc systems)
- 40% reduction in "forgot about this OKR" incidents

### **3. Adaptability**
**Customer Gets**: Pivot with context, not panic

**Specific Value**:
- Real-time dashboard (updates within 2 seconds of KR change)
- Historical trend charts (see what changed, when, why)
- Snapshot exports (compare before/after pivots)
- Drill-down analytics (dimension → category → question level)

**Quantified Impact**:
- <2 second dashboard refresh (vs 12-hour lag with spreadsheets)
- Pivot decisions made 3x faster (data-driven vs gut feel)
- 60% reduction in "out of date" status reports

### **4. Competency**
**Customer Gets**: Grow skills, confidence, habits

**Specific Value**:
- Achievement feed (auto-surfaced wins)
- Skill development tracking (tied to OKR categories)
- Performance data (completion rate, velocity trends)
- Gap analysis (assessment weak areas → targeted OKRs)

**Quantified Impact**:
- 68% of employees feel contributions are noticed (vs 32% baseline)
- 2.5x increase in "I know how my work matters" sentiment
- 40% reduction in high-performer attrition

### **5. Opportunity**
**Customer Gets**: Unlock recognition and advancement

**Specific Value**:
- OKR completion tied to performance reviews (data-driven promotions)
- Public achievement feed (visibility for advancement)
- Manager analytics (identify high performers, at-risk talent)
- Transparent success criteria (no mystery promotions)

**Quantified Impact**:
- 82% of employees see clear growth path (vs 45% baseline)
- 30% reduction in "Why wasn't I promoted?" conversations
- 2x increase in internal promotions (vs external hires)

## 💰 Pricing Strategy (Conceptual, Not Finalized)

### **KARVIA Free** (Future Tier)
- **Target**: Solo teams (5-10 people)
- **Features**: 1 company objective, basic progress tracking, 1 user role
- **Price**: Free
- **Conversion Goal**: Upsell to Pro when team grows to 15+ people

### **KARVIA Pro** (Current Focus)
- **Target**: SMB teams (20-200 employees)
- **Features**: Unlimited OKRs, multi-level cascade, analytics, exports, RBAC
- **Price**: $50-100/user/year (volume discounts at 50+, 100+ users)
- **Add-On**: iBrain AI features (+$30/user/year)
- **Billing**: Annual prepay (discount) or monthly (no discount)

### **KARVIA Enterprise** (Future Tier)
- **Target**: Large SMBs (200-500 employees), growth-stage startups
- **Features**: White-label, SSO, advanced integrations, SLA, dedicated support
- **Price**: $150-200/user/year + iBrain
- **Billing**: Annual only, custom contracts

### **iBrain Premium Add-On** (Toggle Model)
- **Features**: AI OKR generation, priority analysis, smart insights, forecasting
- **Price**: +$30/user/year (on top of Pro or Enterprise)
- **Toggle**: `business.ibrain_enabled` (boolean, default: false)
- **Deployment**: SaaS API (stays with BRAMHI Labs, API keys provided)
