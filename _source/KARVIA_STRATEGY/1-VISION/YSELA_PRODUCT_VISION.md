## Quick Summary

YSELA is the **user-facing product layer** that wraps the KARVIA engine.

| Layer | What It Is | User Sees? |
|-------|------------|------------|
| **YSELA** | Product brand, UX, behavior frameworks | Yes |
| **KARVIA** | OKR engine, APIs, data models | No |
| **iBrain** | AI/ML intelligence (future) | No |

---

## What YSELA Owns

- User-facing copy and language
- Behavior frameworks (BBB, GRIT, PBL)
- Coach persona and prompts
- Gamification presentation
- Experience design
