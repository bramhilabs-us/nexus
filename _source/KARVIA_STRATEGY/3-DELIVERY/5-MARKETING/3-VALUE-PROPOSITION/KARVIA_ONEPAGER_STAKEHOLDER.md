## How It Works: The Karvia Journey

### 1. The Consultant-Client Partnership

Karvia enables **business consultants** to serve multiple SMB clients from a single platform. The consultant:

- **Onboards the company** with strategic context (goals, challenges, industry)
- **Sends assessments** to measure organizational health
- **Generates tailored OKRs** using AI insights
- **Tracks progress** across all their clients

This creates a trusted partnership where consultants deliver 3x more value with half the effort.

### 2. The SSI Assessment: Know Before You Plan

Before generating goals, Karvia **diagnoses** organizational health through the **SSI Framework**:

```
    SPEED              STRENGTH           INTELLIGENCE
    How fast you       How well you       How smartly you
    execute            handle challenges  adapt & learn

    ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │                   SSI SCORE: 7.2                    │
    │   Speed: 8.1   │   Strength: 5.5   │   Intel: 7.8   │
    └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

Each dimension is scored through **12 MECE blocks** covering:
- Sales & Revenue Growth
- Product & Market Readiness
- Team Capability & Culture
- Operational Efficiency
- Financial Discipline
- And 7 more...

**The insight**: You can't improve what you can't measure. SSI tells you exactly where your business is strong and where it's vulnerable.

### 3. AI-Generated OKRs: From Assessment to Action

Based on SSI scores and business context, Karvia's AI generates **contextual objectives** that target your actual weaknesses:

```
Objective: Improve Operational Efficiency (Strength: 5.5)
├── Key Result 1: Reduce project delivery time by 25%
├── Key Result 2: Automate 3 manual processes by Q2
└── Key Result 3: Achieve 90% on-time milestone completion
```

**This is not generic.** AI considers:
- Your industry benchmarks
- Company size and stage
- Team capabilities from assessment
- Strategic priorities you've shared

### 4. The Cascade: From Company to Individual

Once approved, objectives **cascade through the organization**:

```
COMPANY OBJECTIVE
    └── Department Key Results
            └── Team Quarterly Goals
                    └── Individual Weekly Goals
                            └── Daily Tasks
```

**Every employee sees exactly 3 things they need to focus on today.**

No confusion. No "What should I prioritize?" questions. Just clarity.

## The Intelligence Layer: AI That Learns

Karvia doesn't just track - it **thinks**.

### Dependency Detection
AI identifies **cross-team dependencies** before they become blockers:
> "Engineering's Q2 goal depends on Design's component library. Design is 2 weeks behind. Suggest: Reallocate resources."

### Industry Benchmarks
Objectives are informed by **industry standards and best practices**:
> "Your SSI Strength score (5.5) is below the tech industry median (6.8). Focus areas: Cash management, talent retention."

### Behavior-Based Assignment
The task tracking engine analyzes **work patterns** to match the right person to the right task:
> "Based on completion velocity and expertise, Alex is 2.3x more likely to deliver this on time than the original assignee."
