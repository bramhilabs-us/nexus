Assessment → Insights → AI OKRs → Cascade → Track → Recognize → Improve

### 4. SSI Framework
- **S**peed - How fast the company executes

### 5. OKR Cascade System
```
Company Objectives (Strategic Goals)
    └── Key Results (Measurable Outcomes)
            └── Quarterly Goals (Team Targets)
                    └── Weekly Goals (Sprint Items)
                            └── Tasks (Daily Actions)
```

## Story Arc Structure

### Act 1: The Problem (Why Now?)
> SMBs are stuck between spreadsheet chaos and enterprise overkill. 70% fail at strategy execution.

### Act 2: The Solution (What We Built)
> Karvia: AI-powered OKR platform that starts with understanding your business (SSI Assessment) and generates objectives targeting your actual weaknesses.

### Act 3: The Journey (How It Works)
> Consultant introduces → Owner runs assessment → AI generates OKRs → Cascade to teams → Daily clarity for employees → Track, recognize, improve.

### Act 4: The Magic (What's Different)
> - AI identifies dependencies between teams
> - Industry benchmarks inform recommendations
> - Behavior analysis matches right person to right task
> - "Top 3 things" for every employee, every day

### Act 5: The Future (Where We're Going)
> iBrain: Predictive planning, natural language coaching
> Culture Score: Team health metrics
> Financial Module: Budget-aware OKRs
