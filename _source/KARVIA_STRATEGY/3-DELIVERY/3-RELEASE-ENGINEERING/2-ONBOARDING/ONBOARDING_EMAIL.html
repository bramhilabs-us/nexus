Welcome to Karvia Business!

Get started with data-driven OKRs in 5 simple steps

1 Select Your Assessment Template
Go to Assessments Hub → My Templates and choose a 50-question template that matches your business (Consulting, Trade, Wellness, etc.)
[Image: Assessment Hub showing template cards with "50 Questions" badges]
2a Create Your Team
Navigate to Teams → Create New Team. Add team name, select a manager, and add initial members.
[Image: Create New Team form with fields]
2b Send Assessment to Team
Click Send to Team on your template, select teams, and click Send Invitations. Team members receive email invites automatically.
[Image: Share Assessment modal with team selection]
3 Take Assessment & See Results
Check Assigned to Me tab, click Take Assessment, answer all questions, and view your SSI scores for Speed, Strength, and Intelligence.
[Image: Assessment Results showing SSI scores - Speed 6.3, Strength 6.1, Intelligence 6.4]
4 Generate AI-Powered Objectives
Go to Objectives → Generate Objective. Select a business category, enable "Assessment Results" as data source, and let AI create your strategic objectives.
[Image: Generate Objective with AI modal]
5 Plan & Track Progress
Break objectives into Key Results → Quarterly Goals → Weekly Goals → Tasks. Use the Planning page for timeline visualization and tracking.
