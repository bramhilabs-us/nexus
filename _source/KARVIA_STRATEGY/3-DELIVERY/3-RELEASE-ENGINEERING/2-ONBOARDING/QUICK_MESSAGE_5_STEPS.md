## Quick Start (Copy-Paste Ready)

**Get started with Karvia in 5 steps:**

1️⃣ **Assessment** → Go to Assessments Hub, pick a 50-question template

3️⃣ **Take It** → Complete your assessment, view your Speed/Strength/Intelligence scores

4️⃣ **Objectives** → Generate AI-powered objectives using your assessment insights

5️⃣ **Plan** → Break into quarterly goals, weekly goals, and tasks. Track progress!
