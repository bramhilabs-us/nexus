# Karvia Business - Quick Start Guide

## Transform Your Business with Data-Driven OKRs

Welcome to Karvia Business! This guide walks you through getting started in 5 simple steps.

---

## Step 1: Select Your Assessment Template

Navigate to **Assessments Hub** and choose a template that matches your business.

### What you'll see:
- **My Templates** tab shows available assessment templates
- Each template displays **50 Questions** with dimension weights (Speed, Strength, Intelligence)
- Choose from industry-specific templates like "Consulting Business Health Assessment"

### Actions:
1. Click **Assessments** in the navigation
2. Select the **My Templates** tab
3. Choose a template (e.g., "Consulting Quick Assessment")
4. Click **Send to Team** or **Send to Company**

![Assessment Hub - Template Selection](assessment-hub-templates.png)

---

## Step 2a: Create Your Team

Before sending assessments, set up your team structure.

### What you'll see:
- "No teams found" message if this is your first time
- Option to **Go to Teams Page**

### Actions:
1. Click **Go to Teams Page** or navigate to **Teams** in the menu
2. Click **Create New Team**
3. Fill in:
   - **Team Name** (e.g., "Sales Team")
   - **Description** (optional)
   - **Department** & **Function**
   - **Team Manager** (select from dropdown)
   - **Initial Members** (check team members)
4. Click **Create Team**

![Create New Team Form](create-team.png)

---

## Step 2b: Send Assessment to Your Team

Now send the assessment invitation to your team members.

### What you'll see:
- **Share Assessment with Teams** modal
- List of your teams with member counts
- Total recipients counter

### Actions:
1. Select teams to receive the assessment (checkbox)
2. Review **Total Recipients** count
3. Click **Send Invitations**
4. Team members receive email invitations automatically

![Share Assessment with Teams](share-assessment-teams.png)

---

## Step 3: Take the Assessment & View Results

Take the assessment yourself and review your SSI scores.

### What you'll see:
- **Assigned to Me** tab shows pending assessments
- Assessment card with template name, question count, and due date
- **Take Assessment** button

### Taking the Assessment:
1. Click **Take Assessment**
2. Answer all 50 questions across Speed, Strength, and Intelligence dimensions
3. Questions include sliders (perception) and dropdown options (metrics)
4. Submit when complete

### Your Results:
After submission, you'll see your **SSI Profile**:
- **Speed Score** (Company Agility & Execution)
- **Strength Score** (Operational Stability)
- **Intelligence Score** (Data-Driven Insights)
- Status indicators: "On Track", "Needs Attention", or "Critical"

![Assessment Results](assessment-results.png)

---

## Step 4: Generate AI-Powered Objectives

Use your assessment insights to create strategic objectives.

### What you'll see:
- **Generate Objective with AI** modal
- Business category options
- Data source checkboxes
- Planning period settings

### Actions:
1. Navigate to **Objectives** page
2. Click **Generate Objective** (AI button)
3. Choose **Business Category** (Growth, Customer Success, Operations, etc.)
4. Select **Data Sources**:
   - Assessment Results (recommended)
   - Company Profile
5. Set **Planning Period** (Start Date + Duration)
6. Click **Generate Objective**

![Generate Objective with AI](generate-objective.png)

---

## Step 5: Plan & Track Your OKRs

Break down objectives into quarterly goals, weekly goals, and tasks.

### The OKR Cascade:
```
Objective (Annual/Custom Period)
  └── Key Results (3-5 measurable outcomes)
        └── Quarterly Goals
              └── Weekly Goals
                    └── Tasks
```

### Actions:
1. Review AI-generated objectives and key results
2. Click into each Key Result to add Quarterly Goals
3. Break Quarterly Goals into Weekly Goals
4. Assign owners and track progress
5. Use **Planning** page for timeline visualization
