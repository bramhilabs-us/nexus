## 2️⃣ Planning Your Quarter (NEW!)

### Sarah Wants to Plan Q1 for Her Sales Team

**Step 1: Click "Planning" in the menu**

**Step 2: See Your Objectives**
```
OBJECTIVE: Reduce Customer Churn to 5%
Current: 15% → Target: 5%

KEY RESULTS:
□ Contact all customers monthly
□ Resolve issues within 24 hours
□ Achieve 90% satisfaction score
```

**Step 3: Click "Create Q1 Plan"**

**Step 4: Watch the Magic Happen! ✨**

The system automatically:
- **Creates 13 weekly milestones** (one for each week in Q1)
  - Week 1: Contact 20 customers
  - Week 2: Contact 20 customers
  - Week 3: Follow up with at-risk accounts
  - ... (continues for 13 weeks)

- **Spreads tasks across weekdays**
  - Monday: 5 customers
  - Tuesday: 5 customers
  - Wednesday: 4 customers
  - Thursday: 4 customers
  - Friday: 2 customers + weekly report
  - *Notice: No more Friday pile-up!*

**Step 5: Assign to Team**
- Tom gets: Customer calls (his strength)
- Mike gets: Issue resolution (technical expert)
- Lisa gets: Satisfaction surveys (great with feedback)

**Time Taken: 15 minutes** (Used to take 3 hours in spreadsheets!)
