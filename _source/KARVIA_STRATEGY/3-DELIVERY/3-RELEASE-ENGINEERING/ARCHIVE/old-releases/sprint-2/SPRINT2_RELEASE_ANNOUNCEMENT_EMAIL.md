# Sprint 2 Release Announcement

**To:** Stakeholders

**Date:** November 20, 2025

Dear Stakeholders,

We're excited to announce that **Sprint 2 (v0.8.0)** has been successfully deployed to production, delivering two game-changing features for daily operations.

## 🎯 What's New

### 1. **Role-Based Dashboard** - Your Daily Command Center
- **Employees** see today's tasks and weekly progress at login
- **Managers** view team performance and goal status
- **Executives** get company-wide metrics and health scores

### 2. **Smart Planning System** - Intelligent Date Management
- Automatic quarterly breakdown (Q1-Q4) with 13-week periods
- Tasks intelligently distributed across weekdays (no more Friday pile-ups!)
- Parent-child date validation prevents scheduling conflicts

---

## 🚶 User Journey Example

**Sarah (Manager) logs in Monday morning:**

1. **Dashboard** shows her team has 5 goals this week, 2 need attention
2. Clicks **Planning** → selects Q1 2025
3. Creates quarterly goal: "Increase customer satisfaction to 95%"
4. System automatically:
   - Sets Q1 dates (Jan 1 - Mar 31)
   - Suggests 13 weekly milestones
   - Distributes 20 tasks across Mon-Fri evenly
5. Team members see their daily tasks immediately on their dashboards

**Time saved: 2 hours of manual planning per week** ⏰
