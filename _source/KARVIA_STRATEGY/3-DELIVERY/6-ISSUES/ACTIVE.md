## How to Add Issues

### Quick Template

```markdown
### BUG-S21-XXX: [Title]
**Priority**: P0 | P1 | P2
**Reported**: 2026-04-01
**Sprint**: 21 (or "Backlog")
**Status**: OPEN

**Description**:
[What's broken?]

**Steps to Reproduce**:
1. Step 1
2. Step 2
3. Step 3

**Expected**: [What should happen]
**Actual**: [What actually happens]

**Files**:
- `path/to/file.js:123`

**Notes**: [Any additional context]
```

### After Resolving

1. Change status to `RESOLVED`
2. Add resolution details
3. Move to [RESOLVED.md](RESOLVED.md)
4. Update sprint view in `by-sprint/SPRINT-XX.md`
