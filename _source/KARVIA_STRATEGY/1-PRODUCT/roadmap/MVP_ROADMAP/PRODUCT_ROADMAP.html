⚠ Scope-Cut Levers Required for $850K Lean

  • NO dedicated QA FTE — folded into team capacity (regression risk ↑)
  • NO PM / Delivery Lead — Tech Lead + Founder absorb coordination
  • NO quarterly threat models — one-time workshop only
  • NO multi-provider AI failover — OpenAI single primary
  • NO bug bounty program (enterprise sales friction)
  • NO cyber + D&O insurance
  • Smaller US pen test firms ($25K not $40K each)
  • DataDog → Grafana/Loki self-hosted (saves $15K, eats 2-3 dev-months)
  • Defer GDPR auto-deletion API to phase 2
  • Defer audit-log retrofit to phase 2
  • Single non-prod environment (no dev + staging separation)
  • Founder absorbs on-call overflow + PM tasks (60-100 hr/mo)

Four-Number Reconciliation

Investor deck headline $300K
Lean (mixed offshore) $850K
Comfortable + AI (recommended) $1.05M
Safe — all gaps closed $1.20M
Cumulative-risk math: with 18 identified risks, even a 28% hit-rate (5 firing) consumes the buffer at any tier. $850K = aggressive bet, 35-45% land rate. $1.05M = balanced, 70-80% land rate. $1.20M = the number to commit to if overrun is unacceptable. Real TCO includes $180K founder oversight not in any line item.
Critical reading: (1) Business growth drives variable costs — if Stage 1 hits 100 Businesses (not 30) by Sep '26, AI tokens 3×. (2) Senior DevOps non-negotiable — mid-DevOps cannot lead AWS migration; this is where projects die quietly. (3) PM/Delivery Lead absence is the #1 hidden cost in $850K min — founder absorbs 60-100 hr/mo of coordination. (4) US-only saves ~$40-60K vs EU dual-residency but caps EU customers in Year 1 (intentional). (5) Beta in June will eat 1-2 wk of July hardening capacity — Mid Dev swing role is the buffer. (6) No SOC 2 in this tier — Beta SOC demand = tier upgrade to Scale ($650-800K incremental), not PR overrun.
