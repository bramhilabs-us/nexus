## Evolution Timeline

### Phase 1: Direct Sales Model (2025)

**Original Plan**:
- Launch January 31, 2026
- Target: SMBs (50-500 employees)
- Channel: Direct sales + marketing
- Pricing: $12/user/month

**Market Sizing**:
- TAM: $112B (Global SMBs)
- SAM: $9.7B (English-speaking, tech-forward)
- SOM Year 1: $32.4M ARR

---

### Phase 2: Consultant-Led Distribution (Early 2026)

**Key Insight**:
Consultants have existing trust relationships with SMBs. Leveraging this trust accelerates adoption and reduces CAC.

**Strategy Shift**:
- From: Direct sales to businesses
- To: Partner with consultants who bring their clients

**Benefits**:
| Factor | Direct Sales | Consultant-Led |
|--------|--------------|----------------|
| CAC | $3,000-5,000 | $500-1,000 |
| Sales Cycle | 3-6 months | 2-4 weeks |
| Trust Level | Build from zero | Pre-established |

---

### Phase 3: Beta Model (April 2026)

**Current Approach**:
> "Land through consultants, expand through executives, retain through employee value."

**Beta Launch**: April 17, 2026

**The 4-Step Consultant Model**:
1. **Assess** - Run SSI diagnostic with client
2. **Team** - Form team, assign positions
3. **Objectives** - Set business + human objectives
4. **Tasks** - Execute through daily work

**What Changed**:
- Timeline (Jan → April)
- Branding (Karvia → YSELA powered by KARVIA)
- Positioning (OKR platform → behavior operating system)

**What Stayed the Same**:
- Consultant-led distribution
- SMB target market
- Trust-based adoption
