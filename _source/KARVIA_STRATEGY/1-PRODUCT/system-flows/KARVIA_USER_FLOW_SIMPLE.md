# How Karvia Works: From Start to Daily Success
*A Simple Story of Business Growth*

---

## 🏢 Meet ABC Consulting
A 50-person consulting firm that wants to grow smarter, not just harder.

---

## The Journey: 7 Simple Steps

### Step 1: "How healthy is our business?" 📋
**What happens:** The owner takes a 20-minute assessment
- Answers questions about speed (how fast they deliver)
- Answers questions about strength (how stable they are)
- Answers questions about intelligence (how well they use data)

**Result:** ABC Consulting scores:
- Speed: 72/100 (Pretty good!)
- Strength: 45/100 (Needs work)
- Intelligence: 81/100 (Excellent!)

*Think of it like a health checkup at the doctor, but for your business.*

---

### Step 2: "Let's get our people organized" 👥
**What happens:** The owner creates teams in the system
- Sales Team (5 people) - Led by Sarah
- Delivery Team (15 people) - Led by Mike
- Operations Team (8 people) - Led by Jessica

**Result:** Everyone knows who they work with and who their manager is.

*Like creating WhatsApp groups, but for work goals.*

---

### Step 3: "What should we focus on?" 🤖
**What happens:** Karvia's AI looks at the assessment scores
- Sees that "Strength" is weak (45/100)
- Automatically suggests 3 objectives to improve it:
  1. "Reduce customer churn from 15% to 5%"
  2. "Improve employee retention to 90%"
  3. "Build 6-month cash reserves"

**Result:** Clear goals created in 1 minute (vs 3 hours of meetings!)

*Like how Netflix recommends shows, but for business goals.*

---

### Step 4: "Let's make it official" 🎯
**What happens:** The owner reviews and approves objectives
- Each objective gets broken into 3-4 "Key Results" (measurable targets)
- Example: "Reduce churn to 5%" becomes:
  - Call every customer monthly
  - Fix issues within 24 hours
  - Send satisfaction surveys weekly

**Result:** Big goals now have clear, measurable steps.

*Like breaking "lose weight" into "walk 10,000 steps daily".*

---

### Step 5: "Who does what, and when?" 📅
**What happens:** Managers use the Planning page
- Sarah (Sales Manager) takes the objective "Reduce churn to 5%"
- Clicks "Create Plan for Q1 2025"
- System automatically:
  - Creates 13 weekly milestones (one per week in the quarter)
  - Suggests daily tasks spread Monday-Friday
  - Sarah assigns tasks to her 5 team members

**Time taken:** 15 minutes (used to take 3 hours in Excel!)

**Result:**
- Quarterly goal → 13 weekly goals → 65 daily tasks
- Each person gets 2-3 tasks per day
- No more "everything due on Friday"!

*Like how Google Maps breaks a long trip into turn-by-turn directions.*

---

### Step 6: "What do I do today?" 📱
**What happens:** Monday morning, everyone logs in

**Tom (Sales Rep) sees his dashboard:**
- **Today's Tasks (3):**
  1. ✓ Call 5 customers from the "at-risk" list
  2. ✓ Update CRM with call notes
  3. ✓ Send follow-up email to yesterday's leads
- **This Week:** 15 tasks total, 3 done
- **Why This Matters:** "These calls reduce churn → Improves company strength → We grow sustainably"

**Sarah (Manager) sees her dashboard:**
- **Team Status:** 3 people on track, 1 needs help, 1 ahead
- **Weekly Goal:** 25 customer calls (40% complete)
- **Alert:** "Mike hasn't logged in for 2 days"

**Owner sees executive dashboard:**
- **Company Health:** Strength improving from 45 → 52
- **Q1 Progress:** 35% complete (on track)
- **Top Performer:** Sarah's team (78% of goals met)

**Result:** Everyone knows exactly what to do, no morning meetings needed!

*Like how your fitness app shows today's workout - clear, simple, motivating.*

---

### Step 7: "Are we getting better?" 📈
**What happens:** After 3 months
- System shows churn dropped from 15% to 11%
- Strength score improved from 45 to 58
- Team completed 78% of all tasks

**Result:** Clear proof that the plan is working!

*Like how your fitness tracker shows you've lost 10 pounds - measurable progress.*
