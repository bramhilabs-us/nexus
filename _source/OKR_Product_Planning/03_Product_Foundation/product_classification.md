# Karvia OKR Product Classification

## Persona Matrix
| Role | Primary Needs | Key Features |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Executive / Business Owner | Strategic visibility, risk & sustainability insights | Scenario dashboards, sentiment narrative, value weighting controls |
| Manager | Team motivation, workload balance, conflict resolution | Action stack, wellbeing badges, planning cockpit |
| Individual Contributor | Clarity, recognition, minimal friction | Daily assistant, transparent context chain, feedback loops |
| Admin / Ops | Governance, provisioning, integrations | Tenant management, audit trails, configurable policies |

## Offering Tiers (Draft)
1. **Growth Starter** – Core OKRs, AI planning, manager action stack, up to 50 users.
2. **Scale Professional** – Adds wellbeing analytics, sentiment pipelines, integrations (Slack/Teams), up to 200 users.
3. **Enterprise Inspire** – White-label, scenario planning, API access, advanced compliance.

## Capability Domains
- **Alignment Engine:** Objectives, key results, cascades, strategy mapping.
- **Intelligence Layer:** AI recommendations, explainability, S/S/I metrics, scenario simulation.
- **Human Experience:** Sentiment tracking, recognition, conflict support, wellbeing dashboards.
- **Delivery Platform:** Microservices, multi-tenant config, integrations, observability.

## Release Classification
- **Must-Haves (Release 1):** Technical hardening, manager-focused flows (action stack, planning cockpit improvements), wellbeing indicators, consistent JWT & service discovery.
- **Should-Haves (Release 2):** Recognition & feedback loops, mobile responsive dashboards, sentiment pipeline, explainable AI improvements.
- **Could-Haves (Release 3):** Scenario planner, integration marketplace, full white-label toolkit, advanced automation.

## Success Metrics
- Time-to-alignment: avg steps to confirm weekly plan per manager.
- AI adoption: % recommendations accepted vs edited.
- Wellbeing response rate: weekly pulse participation.
- Retention uplift attributable to recognition & empathy features.
