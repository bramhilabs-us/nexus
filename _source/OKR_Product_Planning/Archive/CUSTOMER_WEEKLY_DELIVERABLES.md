## 🎬 WEEK 1 DELIVERABLE DETAILS (Oct 13-17)

### **Goal**: SSI Assessment System Operational

### **What You'll Get**:

1. **Assessment Template Library**
   - Pre-built SSI assessment (Speed/Strength/Intelligence questions)
   - 47 strategic questions across 3 dimensions
   - Question library for customization

2. **Team Invitation System**
   - Send assessment invitations via email
   - Track invitation status (Pending/Sent/Completed)
   - Set assessment deadlines
   - Send reminder notifications
   - View completion progress

3. **Assessment Taking Experience**
   - Clean, simple survey interface
   - Progress tracking as team responds
   - Save and resume capability
   - Mobile-responsive design

### **What You Can Do This Week**:

✅ **Monday-Tuesday**: System setup
- Your company account created
- Initial admin access configured

✅ **Wednesday**: Testing begins
- You can take the assessment yourself
- See how scoring works
- Test the survey experience

✅ **Thursday-Friday**: Team invitations
- Invite 5-10 team members for pilot
- Send assessment invitations
- Monitor who completes
- See real-time completion tracking

### **Friday Demo (Oct 17 @ 3:00 PM)**:
We'll show you:
1. How to send invitations to your team
2. Live assessment completion tracking
3. Reminder system for pending assessments
4. Preview of Week 2 (Results Dashboard)

### **Success Criteria**:
- ✅ You can invite team members
- ✅ Team receives email invitations
- ✅ Team can complete assessments
- ✅ You can track completion status
- ✅ System captures all responses
