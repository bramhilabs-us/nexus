## 🚀 Get Started in 3 Simple Steps (15 minutes)

### **Step 1: Create Your Account** (2 min)

1. Visit: **[https://karvia-business.onrender.com/pages/signup.html](https://karvia-business.onrender.com/pages/signup.html)**
2. Fill in:
   - Your name and email
   - Company name
   - Password (e.g., `Test1234!` - min 8 chars with uppercase, lowercase, number, special character)
   - **Role**: Select **"Manager"**
3. Click "Create Account"
4. ✅ You're in!

---

### **Step 2: Create & Send Your First Assessment** (3 min)

1. You'll land on the **Assessment Hub** automatically
2. Click **"Create New Assessment"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name**: "My First Assessment"
   - **Description**: "Testing strategic alignment"
   - **Template**: "SSI Strategic Alignment Assessment (47 Questions)"
   - **Duration**: "7 days"
4. Click **"Create Assessment"**
5. Click **"Send Invitation"** on your new assessment
6. **Send to yourself** (so you can test the full flow):
   - Enter your email, first name, last name
   - Click "Send Invitation"
7. ✅ Check your inbox!

---

### **Step 3: Take Assessment & See Results** (10-15 min)

1. Open the email: **"Assessment Invitation from Karvia Business"**
2. Click the secure link
3. Complete the **47-question survey**:
   - Rate statements from 1 (Strongly Disagree) to 5 (Strongly Agree)
   - Covers 8 areas: Vision, Strategy, Execution, People, Performance, Innovation, Stakeholders, Governance
   - Be honest - no wrong answers!
4. Click **"Submit Assessment"**
5. **View your results instantly**:
   - Overall strategic alignment score (0-100%)
   - Visual radar chart across 8 categories
   - Strengths and improvement areas
   - Detailed category breakdowns

**That's it!** 🎉 You've completed your first strategic alignment assessment.

## 🆘 Need Help?

**FAQs**:

**Q: Didn't receive invitation email?**
A: Check spam folder. Ask sender to resend from Assessment Hub.

**Q: Can I pause the assessment?**
A: Yes! Progress auto-saves. Use the same link to continue.

**Q: Can I change answers after submitting?**
A: No, submissions are final. Review carefully before submitting.

**Q: Who sees my results?**
A: Only you and the person who invited you.
