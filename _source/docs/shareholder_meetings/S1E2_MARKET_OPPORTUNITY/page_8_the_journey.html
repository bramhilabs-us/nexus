The Journey

Spark Digital
Marketing Agency
Team Size 18 employees
Account Managers 5 AMs
Founder Lisa (overworked)
Revenue $3.2M
The Symptom
"Every client hiccup lands on my desk. Not because they can't handle it — because they don't trust themselves to."
The YSELA Journey
🔍
1. Assess
"Where is trust leaking?"
SSI reveals AMs score 38 on Strength.
They know what to do — they just don't feel safe deciding.
👥
2. Discover
"Who are these people?"
Interest survey reveals: Marcus loves hiking, Priya loves cooking, Aisha craves deep conversations.
🎯
3. Aim
"What would safe feel like?"
"Help AMs feel confident enough to say 'I've got this' — and mean it."
📅
4. Plan
"Design the conditions"
Client escalations discussed on a 1-hour hike. Decision ownership tracked visibly.
🎉
5. Play
"Make it visible"
Friday ritual: "Decision of the Week". Celebrate the act of owning, not just the outcome.
Culture Conditions
🥾
Walking Escalations
Wednesdays, 7:30am
Hard client conversations happen on the trail. Side-by-side, moving, no screens.
Why it works: Movement reduces cortisol. Side-by-side feels less confrontational.
🍽
Monthly Potluck
First Friday
"Wins & Misses" — share one decision that worked, one that didn't. While eating together.
Why it works: Breaking bread triggers oxytocin. Vulnerability becomes normal.
💬
Deep Talk Hour
Bi-weekly (optional)
For those who need to process. No agenda. Just space to think out loud.
Why it works: Some people need depth to feel safe. This honors that.
The Outcome
Lisa: 2+ hrs/day on AM decisions 30 min/day
AMs: "Should I check first?" "I've got this"
New hires: "Who's in charge?" "We own things"
