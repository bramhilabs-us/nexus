# Karvia OKR User Stories

## Document Overview
- **Purpose:** Capture end-to-end user stories that describe how small-to-medium business stakeholders (20–200 employees) engage with the Karvia OKR platform to foster alignment, empathy, and sustainable growth.
- **Audience:** Product, design, engineering, and enablement teams translating vision into experiences and delivery milestones.
- **Dependencies:**
  - `docs/MASTER_IMPLEMENTATION_GUIDE.md` for architectural baseline.
  - `docs/code-review/release-2024-09-27.md` for current technical gaps that influence feasibility.
  - `Karvia_OKR_Mockups/` for UI flow references (to be iteratively updated against these stories).
- **Relevance (September 2024 Release):** Guides prioritisation of features that reduce cross-team friction, embed dynamic values (speed, strength, intelligence), and power LLM-driven insights.

## Shared Tenets
- Objectives, key results, weekly goals, and tasks are centrally defined and dynamically configurable—no hard-coded values across services.
- AI copilots surface human-centric prompts: empathetic communication, conflict resolution, and sustainable decision nudges.
- Continuous measurement of Speed/Strength/Intelligence informs every role’s view and shapes recommendations.
- Transparency first: every user understands how their work ladders into the company’s mid- and long-term objectives.

---

## Executive Leadership (Founders, C-Suite)
### Primary Goals
- Maintain clarity on strategic direction, risk posture, and cultural health.
- Ensure departmental objectives reinforce long-term mission and responsible growth.

### User Stories
1. *As an executive,* I want a daily strategic pulse that blends OKR progress with LLM summarised sentiment so I can intervene before misalignment escalates.
2. *As an executive,* I need to review cascading objectives and approve quarterly shifts with visibility into downstream impacts, so that teams stay aligned without rework.
3. *As an executive,* I want sustainability flags when objectives bias short-term gains over long-term resilience, so I can rebalance priorities.
4. *As an executive,* I need configurable guardrails on Speed/Strength/Intelligence thresholds, so the platform adapts to evolving strategy without code changes.

### Value Delivered
- Faster executive decisions rooted in both numbers and human context.
- Reduced friction caused by ambiguous priorities or value drift.

---

## Department & Team Managers
### Primary Goals
- Coordinate teams around quarterly milestones while preserving morale and collaboration.
- Detect and resolve blockers or interpersonal friction early.

### User Stories
1. *As a manager,* I want a morning briefing that highlights my team’s OKR progress, dependencies, and emotional tone, so I can plan conversations that matter.
2. *As a manager,* I need the system to alert me when objectives conflict or resource plans overload people, so I can reassign work proactively.
3. *As a manager,* I want AI-generated coaching prompts tailored to individual motivations, so my 1:1s are empathetic and actionable.
4. *As a manager,* I need to adjust departmental KPIs and value weightings without engineering support, so we stay responsive to market shifts.

### Value Delivered
- Managers spend less time reconciling spreadsheets and more time nurturing teams.
- Conflict mitigation becomes structured, timely, and emotionally intelligent.

---

## Individual Contributors / Employees
### Primary Goals
- Understand personal objectives in context of company mission.
- Reduce administrative overhead and surface meaningful collaboration cues.

### User Stories
1. *As an employee,* I want a weekly digest translating executive goals into my tasks, so I never lose sight of the bigger picture.
2. *As an employee,* I need frictionless status updates that auto-summarise outcomes, so my energy goes into impact rather than reporting.
3. *As an employee,* I want to flag interpersonal friction safely, receiving guidance on constructive dialogue, so team trust improves.
4. *As an employee,* I need reflective prompts that celebrate progress and suggest balance, so burnout risk is minimised.

### Value Delivered
- Higher engagement through purpose-driven transparency.
- Seamless collaboration with psychological safety reinforced.

---

## Business Owners / Directors
### Primary Goals
- Balance growth, profitability, and cultural sustainability across the organisation.
- Validate that OKR investments translate into long-term strategic strength.

### User Stories
1. *As a business owner,* I want scenario planning that shows Speed/Strength/Intelligence trade-offs for proposed objectives, so I can invest responsibly.
2. *As a business owner,* I need to audit historical OKR performance alongside financial indicators, so I can calibrate future bets.
3. *As a business owner,* I want configurable value frameworks (e.g., sustainability, community impact) feeding OKR scoring, so the system aligns with our ethos.
4. *As a business owner,* I need insight into talent well-being metrics tied to objectives, so I can reinforce a giving, humane culture.

### Value Delivered
- Strategic decisions incorporate both quantitative and qualitative health signals.
- The platform becomes a partner in crafting a resilient, human-centred future.
