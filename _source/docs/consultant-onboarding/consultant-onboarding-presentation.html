Stage 1

Assessments and Focus Choice

Page objective

Create a trustworthy baseline for Speed, Strength, and Intelligence.

Consultant actionLaunch assessments and frame why each dimension matters.
Co-creation actionAgree which gap to address first this quarter.
Expected outputPriority area selected and ready for team design.
Stage 2

Build Teams Around the Chosen Direction

Page objective

Translate strategy into accountable team structures.

Consultant actionAssign contributors, define ownership, and confirm team health.
Co-creation actionIdentify cross-functional dependencies early.
Expected outputTeam map aligned to objective scope.
Stage 3

Arrive Together at Strategic Objectives

Page objective

Finalize strategic objectives with measurable key results.

Consultant actionFacilitate trade-offs and confirm objective quality.
Co-creation actionLink each objective to owner, timeline, and risk status.
Expected outputA committed objective portfolio for execution.
Stage 4 and 5

Plan the Journey, Then Run Daily Tasks

Page objective

Break objectives into approved weekly plans and daily task execution.

Consultant actionReview AI planning suggestions and approve the weekly sequence.
Manager actionUse the task board for daily focus, progress updates, and reassignments.
Expected outputExecution rhythm with visible throughput and fewer coordination gaps.
