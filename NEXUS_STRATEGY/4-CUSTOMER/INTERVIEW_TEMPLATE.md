# Interview / instrument session template

> Copy per session. One file per session, named `interviews/<client>/<day>-<role>-<n>.md`. Evidence lives in Nexus (assessment module); this template is its structured capture form.

## Session header

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Client / program | |
| Sprint day · instrument | e.g., Day 2 · Operations deep dive (walk the floor) |
| Participant role | CEO / dept head / team lead / employee — per the ~35-person matrix |
| Interviewer · duration | |
| Consent for workforce-profile signals | yes / no |

## Capture discipline (read before, every time)

- **Journey is the input** — ask about how their work actually flows (steps, handoffs, waits, rework); the AIR dimensions are computed later, never asked directly. No "how would you rate your data maturity?"
- **Observe > self-report** — walk the floor; record what you saw next to what they said. Most assessments fail because consultants assume (Day 9 exists to de-bias; capture raw here).
- **Verbatims are gold** — quote, don't paraphrase, when something is load-bearing.

## Capture

### The work as it flows
*(steps, systems touched, handoffs, approvals, waiting, rework — the Value Stream raw material)*

### Friction points
*(each one: what / how often / cost in time / who else it touches)*

### "Perfect digital employee" answer
*(the Day 8 anchor question — what would they delegate first, verbatim)*

### Observed vs said
*(what the floor showed that the interview didn't)*

| Output | Value | Notes |
